Welcome!

This app was developed to provide users a way of navigating the University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics(UIHC) campus. The campus is a mile long from the Boyd Tower to the Pomerantz Family Pavillion. The entire campus is millions of square feet in area with over 16,000 staff and over a million annual visits. Navigating the hospital is a challenging task for newcomers and experienced hospital goers alike. With our app, we hope to alleviate the stress of navigating the hospital with detailed directions between any two points.

Currently our application only supports navigation on the first floor from the Boyd Tower to the Pappajohn Pavillion. We hope to expand the supported locations in the future.
